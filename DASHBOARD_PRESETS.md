## How to Use Presets

1. Click the settings icon (‚öôÔ∏è) in the top-right corner
2. Navigate to the "Presets" section
3. Browse through the preset cards
4. Click "Apply" on your chosen preset
5. Your dashboard will instantly transform

## Customization After Applying

After applying a preset, you can:
- **Drag widgets** to reposition them
- **Resize widgets** by dragging corners
- **Add more widgets** from the Widget Gallery (+)
- **Remove widgets** using the X button
- **Configure widgets** using their settings icon
- Your layout is **automatically saved**

## Creating Your Own Layout

If none of the presets fit your needs:
1. Start with the closest preset
2. Add/remove widgets from the Widget Gallery
3. Arrange them to your preference
4. Configure each widget's settings
5. Your custom layout is saved automatically
